About ARUNODAYA HIGH SCHOOL KAMAKSHIPURA

ARUNODAYA HIGH SCHOOL KAMAKSHI PUR was established in 1992 and it is managed by the Private Aided society. The school is located in a rural area inside the NORTH4 block of BENGALURU U NORTH district of Karnataka. It provides education for Grades 8 to 10, is co-educational, and conducts instruction in Kannada. The academic session begins in April each year.

Infrastructure & facilities

The school operates from a rented building and has 8 classrooms in good condition, plus 2 additional rooms for non-teaching activities and a separate room for the Headmaster/Teacher. The campus has no boundary wall but is approachable via an all-weather road. Electricity connection is available and tap water is the functional source of drinking water.

Sanitation: 1 boys toilet (functional) and 1 girls toilet (functional). The school has a playground and a library containing 360 books. There are no computers or a computer-aided learning lab at present. The school does not require a ramp to access classrooms.

Mid-day meal: Provided but not prepared on school premises.
